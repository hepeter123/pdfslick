Why Choose PNG over JPEG?

PNG (Portable Network Graphics) uses lossless compression, meaning no image data is discarded during conversion. This makes PNG the superior choice for documents with text, diagrams, charts, CAD drawings, screenshots, and any content where crisp edges matter. Unlike JPEG, PNG also supports full alpha-channel transparency — perfect when you need to overlay a PDF page onto another image or place it on a non-white background. For archival purposes, PNG guarantees that every pixel of the original rendering is preserved exactly.

Adjustable Render Scale for Any Use Case

PDFSlick offers three render scale settings to match your needs. The 1x scale produces images at the PDF's native resolution — ideal for web use and quick previews where smaller file sizes matter. Higher scales produce larger files, so choose the level that fits your workflow.
