AI Paper Reader — Understand Any Academic Paper in Plain Language

Reading academic papers is one of the biggest challenges for researchers, students, and professionals worldwide. PDFSlick's AI Paper Reader goes beyond simple translation — it helps you truly understand complex research by providing three complementary tools: plain-language explanations, precise academic translations, and rewrite suggestions for citations.

More Than Translation — True Understanding

Traditional translation tools translate word by word, often producing awkward output with academic writing. Our AI Paper Reader understands the context, methodology, and findings before producing explanations. The plain-language mode explains complex concepts using everyday words and analogies, while academic translation preserves technical terminology with original English terms in parentheses.

Highlight Any Passage, Get Instant Understanding

Simply highlight any text in the PDF viewer and choose what you need: a plain explanation, an academic translation, or a rewrite suggestion for your own paper. Each explanation card stays in your sidebar for reference, and you can ask follow-up questions about any passage.

Key Features

Split-Screen Reader: PDF on the left, AI assistant on the right. Drag the divider to adjust. Auto Summary: Upload a paper and get an instant plain-language overview with key terms explained. Three Understanding Modes: Translate (academic precision), Explain (plain language), Rewrite (for your citations). Follow-up Chat: Ask anything about the paper. Export Notes: Download all your understanding cards and conversations as a PDF.

Supported Languages

PDFSlick AI Paper Reader supports understanding in seven languages: Chinese (Simplified), Japanese, Korean, Spanish, Portuguese, French, and German.

Who Is This For?

Graduate students reading papers outside their native language. Researchers keeping up with international publications. Medical professionals reviewing clinical studies. Engineers exploring cross-disciplinary research. Anyone who wants to understand — not just read — academic papers.

Privacy & Free Usage

Your paper text is sent to our secure AI server for analysis and returned to your browser. We do not store papers after processing. Every user gets one free paper per day with free explanations — no registration required.
