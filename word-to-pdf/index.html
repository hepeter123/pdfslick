Why Convert Word Documents to PDF?

PDF is the universal standard for document sharing. Unlike Word files that can look different depending on the viewer's software, fonts, and operating system, PDFs render identically everywhere — on any device, any operating system, any PDF reader. Converting your Word documents to PDF ensures your formatting, fonts, tables, and layout appear exactly as you intended, whether the recipient opens the file on a Windows PC, Mac, iPhone, or Android tablet.
